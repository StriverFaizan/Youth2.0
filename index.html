Introduction to Algebra

Welcome to the fascinating world of algebra! In this chapter, we'll explore the fundamental concepts that form the foundation of algebraic thinking.

What is Algebra?

Algebra is a branch of mathematics that uses letters and symbols to represent numbers and quantities in formulas and equations. It's essentially a way to solve mathematical problems by finding the value of unknown variables.

Key Concepts We'll Cover:

  • Variables and constants
  • Expressions and equations
  • Solving linear equations
  • Application of algebra in real life
